Data Collection

UNMUTE collects no data whatsoever. We do not collect your name, email, location, usage statistics, or any other personal information.

What Stays on Your Device

  • All alarm data (times, labels, repeat schedules) is stored locally using SwiftData on your device only.
  • Alarm sounds are bundled with the app and never uploaded anywhere.
  • Notification permissions are managed by iOS and are never shared with us.
  • Timezone preferences are stored locally and never transmitted.
